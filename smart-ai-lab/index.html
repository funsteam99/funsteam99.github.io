What is SMART AI Lab?

SMART AI Lab helps children move beyond simply using AI tools. Students learn what AI is, how it works in daily life, how to ask better questions, how to create with AI, and how to use it safely and responsibly.

SMART AI Lab 不只是讓孩子接觸 AI 工具，而是帶孩子一步步理解 AI、清楚提問、動手創作，並建立安全與負責任的使用觀念。

Frequently Asked Questions

Will this course make children depend too much on AI?

No. We teach students how to question AI, verify information, spot mistakes, and use tools responsibly instead of depending on them blindly.

Does my child need coding experience?

No. Beginner levels do not require coding experience. Students can start with AI literacy, creativity, and communication, then move into coding-related AI projects later.

How is this different from just trying AI tools at home?

SMART AI Lab is structured, age-appropriate, and guided by teachers. Students do not only try tools—they learn how AI works, how to think critically, and how to use it responsibly.
